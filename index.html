Salmiya Grand Hotel

Salmiya Grand Hotel is a 3-star hotel located on Al-Dimna Street at the heart of Salmiya, Kuwait City. Each elegant room is designed to make you feel at home with our hospitality team available to serve you around the clock.

The Hotel is ideally situated on the shores of the Arabian Gulf, located just a short 20 minutes drive from Kuwait International Airport, and within easy reach to Kuwait's prime business, shopping and entertainment destinations.

An array of outstanding dining options are on offer, including complementary breakfast with every booking. The hotel’s welcoming lobby and outdoor seating area provides the perfect place for coffee breaks and meetings.

Salmiya Grand Hotel welcomes celebrations at The Grand Ballroom, from weddings to meetings and conferences. With our buffet catering services, the ballroom may be tailored to meet your specific needs.
